About IEEE

Over 420,000 individuals in more than 160 countries are members of IEEE, one-forth of which is made up of undergraduate and graduate students. For over 130 years, IEEE members have helped shape the world's technology future. IEEE and its members inspire a global community to innovate for a better tomorrow.
